# Founder Voice Interview Template

Use these questions when voice samples are limited. Can be conducted via call, email, or form.

## Communication Style

1. How would you describe your communication style in 3 words?

2. When writing professionally, are you more:
   - Direct and to-the-point
   - Diplomatic and nuanced
   - Casual and conversational
   - Data-driven and analytical

3. Do you prefer short sentences or longer, flowing ones?

## Content Preferences

4. What 3 topics could you talk about for hours without getting bored?

5. What's a common belief in your industry that you disagree with?

6. What advice do you find yourself giving repeatedly?

7. What questions do people always ask you?

## Personality & Tone

8. How do you feel about:
   - Using humor in professional content?
   - Sharing personal stories or failures?
   - Using emojis?
   - Taking strong stances?

9. On a scale of 1-10, how vulnerable are you willing to be? (1 = only wins, 10 = share failures openly)

10. Do you prefer to sound like an expert teaching, or a peer sharing?

## Language Patterns

11. What phrases do you catch yourself saying often? (In meetings, emails, conversations)

12. Are there any words or phrases that feel "not you"?

13. Do you use industry jargon freely, or do you prefer plain language?

## Influences & Anti-Influences

14. Name 2-3 people whose LinkedIn presence you admire. What specifically do you like?

15. What type of LinkedIn content makes you cringe or unfollow?

16. Is there anyone you specifically don't want to sound like?

## Hot Takes

17. What's your spiciest take that might be controversial in your industry?

18. What's something "everyone knows" in your industry that's actually wrong?

19. What trend is overhyped? What's underrated?

## Closing

20. If you could only post about one thing on LinkedIn for the next year, what would it be?

21. What do you want people to think/feel after reading your posts?

22. Anything else that captures "how you talk" that we should know?
