# Platform Selection Matrix

Use this matrix to determine platform priority for each client.

## Platform Priority by Business Type

| Business Type | Instagram | Facebook | GBP | LinkedIn |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----|----------|
| B2C Service (music, fitness, beauty) | **Primary** | Secondary | High | Skip |
| B2B Service (consulting, agencies) | Secondary | **Primary** | Medium | Consider |
| Retail/Local (restaurants, shops) | **Primary** | **Primary** | **High** | Skip |
| Professional Services (legal, medical) | Secondary | **Primary** | **High** | Consider |
| Home Services (plumbing, HVAC) | Secondary | **Primary** | **High** | Skip |
| Healthcare/Wellness | Secondary | **Primary** | **High** | Skip |

## Platform Characteristics

### Instagram
**Best for:** Visual businesses, younger demographics, brand building
**Posting frequency:** 4-5x/week
**Content types:** Carousels (primary), single images, monthly Reel
**Audience:** 18-44, visual-first consumers
**Strengths:** Engagement, brand awareness, visual storytelling
**Weaknesses:** Link limitations, algorithm changes

### Facebook
**Best for:** Community building, local reach, older demographics
**Posting frequency:** 3-4x/week
**Content types:** Images, links, events, community posts
**Audience:** 35+, local community members
**Strengths:** Local targeting, groups, events, reviews
**Weaknesses:** Declining organic reach, younger audience exodus

### Google Business Profile
**Best for:** Local SEO, trust building, service businesses
**Posting frequency:** 2-3x/week
**Content types:** Updates, offers, events, products
**Audience:** High-intent searchers
**Strengths:** Direct impact on local search, reviews, maps presence
**Weaknesses:** Limited engagement, no community features

### LinkedIn
**Best for:** B2B, professional services, thought leadership
**Posting frequency:** 2-3x/week (if included)
**Content types:** Articles, professional updates, case studies
**Audience:** Business professionals, decision makers
**Strengths:** Professional credibility, B2B leads
**Weaknesses:** Not suitable for most B2C businesses

## Decision Framework

### Make Instagram Primary When:
- Visual product/service (beauty, fitness, food, design)
- Target audience under 45
- Brand building is key goal
- Content is highly visual

### Make Facebook Primary When:
- Local service business
- Target audience over 35
- Community building is key goal
- Events are important to business

### Prioritize GBP When:
- Any local business
- Service area business
- Reviews are important
- "Near me" searches are relevant

## Posting Frequency Guidelines

| Priority Level | Instagram | Facebook | GBP |
|----------------|-----------|----------|-----|
| Primary | 5x/week | 4x/week | 3x/week |
| Secondary | 3-4x/week | 3x/week | 2x/week |
| Tertiary | 2x/week | 2x/week | 1x/week |
