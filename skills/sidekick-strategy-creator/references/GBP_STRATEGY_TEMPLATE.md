## Post Formatting

### Anatomy of a GBP Post
1. **Hook** (first line - attention grabber)
2. **Body** (100-300 words, value-focused)
3. **CTA** (what to do next)
4. **Button** (Learn More / Book / Call / Order)

### Voice on GBP
[Professional, local-focused, helpful - no hashtags, minimal emoji]

### What NOT to Include
- Hashtags (not used on GBP)
- Excessive emojis
- Links in body (use button instead)
- Promotional language without value
