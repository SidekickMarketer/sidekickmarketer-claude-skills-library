# Brand Voice Framework

Use these attributes to define each client's unique voice.

## Voice Attribute Options

### Tone
Select 3-4 that best fit the client:

| Warm Tones | Professional Tones | Energetic Tones |
|------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Friendly | Expert | Enthusiastic |
| Approachable | Authoritative | Motivating |
| Caring | Trustworthy | Inspiring |
| Supportive | Reliable | Exciting |
| Welcoming | Knowledgeable | Passionate |
| Nurturing | Credible | Dynamic |

### Personality Types
Select 1-2 that match the brand:

| Type | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Friendly Neighbor | Warm, helpful, always there | Local service businesses |
| Trusted Advisor | Knowledgeable, guiding, reliable | Professional services |
| Industry Expert | Authoritative, educational, leading | Specialists, consultants |
| Cheerleader | Supportive, celebrating, motivating | Fitness, education |
| Community Hub | Connecting, inclusive, local | Retail, restaurants |
| Calm Guide | Reassuring, patient, steady | Healthcare, wellness |

### Formality Scale

| Level | Language Style | Emoji Use | Example Opening |
|-------|---------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Casual | Conversational, contractions, slang OK | Moderate (2-4) | "You know that feeling when..." |
| Professional | Clear, polished, accessible | Light (1-2) | "Many business owners ask us..." |
| Expert | Technical terms OK, educational | Minimal (0-1) | "Research shows that..." |

## Industry Voice Presets

### Music/Arts Education
```
Tone: Warm, Inspiring, Expert, Encouraging
Personality: The supportive music teacher who celebrates every win
Language: Casual-Professional blend
Emoji: Moderate (music notes, celebration, hearts)
```

### Home Services
```
Tone: Trustworthy, Helpful, Friendly, Reliable
Personality: The skilled neighbor you can always count on
Language: Casual, accessible
Emoji: Light (tools, checkmarks, thumbs up)
```

### Healthcare/Wellness
```
Tone: Caring, Professional, Reassuring, Knowledgeable
Personality: The calm guide helping you feel your best
Language: Professional but warm
Emoji: Minimal (health-related, hearts, stars)
```

### Professional Services
```
Tone: Expert, Trustworthy, Approachable, Insightful
Personality: The advisor who makes complex things simple
Language: Professional, jargon-free
Emoji: Minimal to none
```

## Caption Hook Examples by Voice

### Warm/Friendly Voice
- "This moment made our whole week..."
- "Can we just take a second to celebrate..."
- "You asked, we're answering!"

### Expert/Educational Voice
- "Here's what most people get wrong about..."
- "The #1 mistake we see is..."
- "Let's break this down..."

### Motivating/Inspiring Voice
- "Imagine what's possible when..."
- "This transformation started with one decision..."
- "Your journey starts here..."

### Community/Local Voice
- "Cincinnati, we love you!"
- "Shoutout to our amazing community..."
- "Nothing beats supporting local..."
