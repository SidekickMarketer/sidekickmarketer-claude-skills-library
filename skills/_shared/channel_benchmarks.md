## Instagram Benchmarks

### Engagement Rates by Industry (2025)

| Industry | Avg Engagement Rate | Top Performer Notes |
|----------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Education | 4.2% - 4.52% | 28 posts/week optimal |
| Financial Services | 4.64% | 26 posts/week optimal |
| Entertainment/Media | 3.70% | 13 posts/week optimal |

| Agencies | 3.4% | - |
| All Industries Median | 0.55% - 1.5% | Declining YoY |

**Source:** [Hootsuite 2025 Benchmarks](https://blog.hootsuite.com/average-engagement-rate/)

### Content Format Performance

| Format | Avg Engagement | vs Single Image | Notes |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|-------|
| **Carousels** | 0.55% - 1.92% | +22-38% higher | "Engagement generator star" |
| Single Images | 0.45% - 1.74% | Baseline | Declining performance |
| Reels | 6.92% (feed) | Highest reach | Up from 4.5% in 2023 |
| Mixed Media Carousel | 2.33% | +30% vs image-only | Images + video combined |

**Key Finding:** Carousels are 12% more likely to be saved and 114% more likely to be liked than single images.

**Source:** [SocialInsider 2025](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks/instagram), [Buffer Research](https://buffer.com/resources/do-instagram-carousels-get-more-engagement/)

### Optimal Carousel Slide Count

| Slides | Performance |
|--------|-------------|
| 1-3 | Good engagement, quick drop after 3 |
| 4-7 | Engagement dip |
| **8-10** | **Highest engagement** - only 6% of carousels use all 10 |

**Recommendation:** Either keep it short (3 slides) or go all-in (8-10 slides).

### Caption Length

| Length | Performance |
|--------|-------------|
| **125-150 characters** | **Optimal engagement** |
| 300-500 characters | Good for carousels |
| 2,200 characters | Platform max (truncated at 125) |

**Source:** [HubSpot Character Guide](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/character-count-guide)

### Hashtag Strategy

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Quantity | 5-10 hashtags (not 30) |
| Character length | 21-24 characters per hashtag |
| Mix | Branded (2-3) + Niche (3-4) + Local (2-3) |

**Source:** [Influencer Marketing Hub](https://influencermarketinghub.com/best-length-for-social-media-posts/)

## Facebook Benchmarks

| Industry | Avg Engagement Rate | Optimal Frequency |
|----------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Financial Services | 2.12% | 2 posts/week |
| Entertainment/Media | 1.75% | 2 posts/week |
| Healthcare | 2.22% | 2 posts/week |
| All Industries | 0.5% - 1% | 3-4 posts/week |

**Key Finding:** Less posting = higher engagement on Facebook.

**Source:** [Hootsuite 2025](https://blog.hootsuite.com/social-media-benchmarks/)

### Caption Length

| Length | Performance |
|--------|-------------|
| **40-80 characters** | **Optimal - 66% higher engagement** |
| Under 40 characters | 86% more engagement than longer |
| Over 280 characters | Engagement drops significantly |
| 63,206 characters | Platform max (avoid) |

**Key Finding:** Shorter is dramatically better. Posts get truncated with "See More" which kills engagement.

**Source:** [Jeff Bullas Study](https://www.jeffbullas.com/), [HubSpot](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/character-count-guide)

### Best Posting Times (2025)

| Day | Best Times | Notes |
|-----|------------|-------|
| Monday | 9 AM - 6 PM | Broad window |
| **Wednesday** | 2 PM (14:00) | Peak day |
| Thursday | 1-3 PM | Strong |
| Friday | 1-3 PM | Strong |
| **Sunday** | 2 PM (14:00) | Highest weekend |

**Best Time Overall:** 1-3 PM (especially 2 PM)
**Best Days:** Wednesday, Thursday, Sunday

**Source:** [RecurPost 2025](https://recurpost.com/blog/best-time-to-post-on-facebook/), [Sprout Social](https://sproutsocial.com/insights/best-times-to-post-on-social-media/)

## Google Business Profile Benchmarks

### Posting Frequency

| Frequency | Recommendation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Minimum** | 1x per week |
| **Optimal** | 2-3x per week |
| Maximum useful | Daily (diminishing returns) |

**Key Finding:** Consistency signals to Google your business is active, improving local SEO.

**Source:** [GMB Briefcase](https://gmbbriefcase.com/blog/how-often-should-i-post-on-my-google-business-profile/)

### Post Specifications

| Element | Limit | Optimal |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Text | 1,500 characters | 100-300 characters |
| Images | Up to 10 | 1-3 high quality |
| Image Resolution | - | 1080 x 1080 pixels |
| Video | 30 sec / 100 MB | Short, impactful |

### Engagement Impact

| Element | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Photos on profile | +42% direction requests, +35% website clicks |
| Posts with CTA | Higher conversion |
| Keyword-rich posts | Better local search visibility |
| Regular posting | Signals "active business" to algorithm |

**Key Finding:** GBP posts don't directly affect rankings but increase CTR and engagement signals.

**Source:** [GMB Crush](https://www.gmbcrush.com/google-my-business-posts/), [Rank Math](https://rankmath.com/blog/google-business-profile-posts/)

### Post Types to Use

| Type | When to Use | Expiration |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Update | General news, tips | 7 days |
| Offer | Promotions with dates | Set end date |
| Event | Time-sensitive | After event |
| Product | Feature offerings | 7 days |

### Best Practices

1. **Always include photos** (profiles with photos get 42% more engagement)
2. **Use CTA buttons** (Book, Call, Learn More, etc.)
3. **Include keywords** for local SEO
4. **NO hashtags** (not a social platform)
5. **Post weekly minimum** to show activity
6. **Mix post types** (offers, updates, events)

## Key Takeaways for Sidekick Model

### Instagram Strategy
1. **Carousels dominate** - Use 50-60% carousel content
2. **8-10 slides** perform best if going long
3. **Hook in first 125 characters** (truncation point)
4. **Post Tue-Thu, 4-6 PM** for highest engagement
5. **5-10 hashtags** (not 30)

### Facebook Strategy
1. **Shorter is better** - Keep under 80 characters
2. **Post less frequently** but consistently (3-4x/week)
3. **Questions drive engagement**
4. **Use links** (clickable in captions)
5. **Post Wed/Thu/Sun around 2 PM**

### GBP Strategy
1. **Weekly minimum** posting to signal activity
2. **Always include photos** (+42% engagement)
3. **Use CTA buttons** on every post
4. **Include local keywords**
5. **No hashtags**
