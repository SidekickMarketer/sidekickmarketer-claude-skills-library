# LinkedIn CTA Bank

Platform-specific calls-to-action optimized for LinkedIn's professional audience and algorithm.

## Engagement CTAs (Drive Comments)

### Open-Ended Questions
- "What's your take on this?"
- "What would you add to this list?"
- "Agree or disagree? I want to hear your reasoning."
- "What's your experience with [topic]?"
- "What's the best advice you've received about [topic]?"
- "What am I missing here?"
- "Hot take or common sense? Let me know below."

### Experience Sharing
- "Drop your biggest [topic] lesson in the comments."
- "Share your [topic] story below - I read every comment."
- "What's your unpopular opinion about [topic]?"
- "Tell me about a time when [situation]..."
- "What would you do differently?"

### Debate Starters
- "Which camp are you in: [Option A] or [Option B]?"
- "Is this genius or terrible? Make your case."
- "Change my mind on this."
- "Where do you stand on this?"

## Save/Bookmark CTAs

- "Save this for your next [situation]."
- "Bookmark this for when you need it."
- "Save this framework for later."
- "You'll want this saved for [specific moment]."
- "Keep this handy for [use case]."

## Share/Repost CTAs

- "Repost if this resonates with your network."
- "Know someone who needs to hear this? Tag them."
- "Share this with a [role] who's dealing with [challenge]."
- "Repost to help others avoid this mistake."
- "If this helped you, it might help someone in your network too."

## Follow CTAs

- "Follow for more [topic] insights."
- "Follow along - I share [frequency] posts about [topic]."
- "Hit the follow button for weekly [topic] breakdowns."
- "If you found this valuable, follow for more."
- "I post about [topic] every [day]. Follow to not miss it."

## Conversion CTAs (Lead Generation)

### DM-Based
- "DM me '[keyword]' and I'll send you [resource]."
- "Want the full [template/guide/list]? DM me."
- "Send me a DM if you want to chat about [topic]."
- "DM me if this sounds like your situation."

### Comment-Based
- "Comment '[word]' and I'll send you [resource]."
- "Drop a 'ðŸ™‹' if you want me to send you [thing]."
- "Comment 'interested' and I'll share [resource]."

### Link-Based
- "Link in comments for [resource]."
- "Full breakdown linked below."
- "Grab the free [resource] - link in first comment."
- "Read the full article: [link in comments]"

**Note:** LinkedIn no longer heavily penalizes links (2025 update: +5% reach). You can include links in the post body with short captions, or use the first comment.

## Connection CTAs

- "Send me a connection request - always happy to chat about [topic]."
- "Let's connect if you're working on [thing]."
- "Connect with me if this resonates."

## Newsletter CTAs

- "Subscribe to my newsletter for the deep dive."
- "I break this down further in my weekly newsletter - link in featured."
- "Get this in your inbox every [day] - subscribe via my profile."

## CTA Selection by Goal

| Goal | Best CTA Types |
|------|----------------|
| Algorithm boost | Open-ended questions, debate starters |
| Build authority | Save CTAs, share CTAs |
| Grow audience | Follow CTAs, connection CTAs |
| Generate leads | DM-based, comment-based conversion |
| Drive traffic | Link-based (in comments or post) |

## CTA Selection by Post Type

| Post Type | Best CTAs |
|-----------|-----------|
| Hot take / Opinion | Debate starters, "Change my mind" |
| Framework / How-to | Save CTAs, "Share with someone who needs this" |
| Personal story | Experience sharing, "What's your story?" |
| Industry news | "What's your take?", debate starters |
| Case study | "DM me for details", conversion CTAs |
| Carousel | Save CTAs, repost CTAs |
| Poll | No CTA needed (poll is the engagement) |

## What NOT to Use

- "Do you agree?" (overused, low-effort responses)
- "Like if you..." (feels manipulative)
- "Comment YES if..." (engagement bait)
- "Link in bio" (that's Instagram, not LinkedIn)
- "Double-tap if..." (that's Instagram)
- Excessive emoji CTAs (feels unprofessional)

## Tone Matching

| Founder Tone | CTA Style |
|--------------|-----------|
| Casual/Friendly | "Tell me your story below!" |
| Professional | "I'd welcome your perspective in the comments." |
| Bold/Direct | "Disagree? Prove me wrong." |
| Warm/Approachable | "I'd love to hear your experience with this." |
| Data-Driven | "What patterns have you observed?" |
