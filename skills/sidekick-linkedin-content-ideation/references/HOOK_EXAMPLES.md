# Strong Hook Examples by Type

## Curiosity Gap
- "I just lost a $50K client. Here's what I learned..."
- "Everyone is doing [X] wrong. Here's the right way..."
- "I analyzed 847 [things]. The results surprised me..."

## Bold Statement
- "Unpopular opinion: [Controversial take]"
- "Stop [common practice]. Start [alternative approach]."
- "Hot take: [Industry norm] is dead."

## Vulnerability/Story
- "Three years ago, I was [low point]. Today, I [achievement]."
- "I made a mistake that cost us [consequence]..."
- "My biggest failure taught me [lesson]..."

## Data/Surprise
- "I analyzed 1,000 [things]. Here's what I found..."
- "[X]% of [audience] are doing [surprising thing]."
- "After tracking [metric] for 6 months, I discovered..."

## Direct Value
- "5 [things] I wish I knew before [milestone]"
- "Here's the [framework/system] that [specific result]..."
- "The [specific tactic] that doubled our [metric]:"

## Trend Commentary
- "Everyone's talking about [trend]. Here's what they're missing..."
- "[Recent news/event] changes everything for [audience]. Here's how..."
- "Why [trending topic] is actually about [deeper insight]..."

## Engagement Psychology Triggers

| Trigger | How It Works |
|---------|--------------|
| Pattern interrupts | Unexpected angles, contrarian takes |
| Curiosity gaps | Open loops that demand completion |
| Social proof | FOMO, bandwagon effect |
| Identity signaling | Content that reflects "people like me" |
| Practical value | Immediately actionable insights |
| Emotional resonance | Stories, struggles, wins, vulnerability |
