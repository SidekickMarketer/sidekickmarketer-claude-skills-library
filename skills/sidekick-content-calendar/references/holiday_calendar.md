## Content Planning Tips

1. **Plan 2-4 weeks ahead** for major holidays
2. **Don't force it** - skip holidays that don't fit brand
3. **Be authentic** - avoid performative posts
4. **Consider local events** - regional celebrations matter
5. **Balance promotional** - holidays aren't just sales opportunities
