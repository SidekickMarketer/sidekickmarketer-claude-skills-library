## Topic Variety Rules

1. **Never repeat exact topic** in same month
2. **Rotate angles** - same pillar, different approach
3. **Match to season** - timely and relevant
4. **Consider special dates** - holidays, awareness days
5. **Balance specificity** - mix broad and niche topics
