# Hashtag Banks by Industry & Platform

## Platform Guidelines

| Platform | Quantity | Placement | Notes |
|----------|----------|-----------|-------|
| Instagram | 5-10 | End of caption or first comment | Mix sizes |
| Facebook | 1-3 | End of caption | Less is more |
| GBP | 0 | N/A | No hashtags |

## Hashtag Mix Formula

For Instagram (5-10 total):
- 2-3 Branded (your business)
- 3-4 Niche (industry-specific)
- 2-3 Local (city/region)

## Hashtag Research Tips

1. **Check hashtag size** - avoid only mega-popular tags
2. **Mix sizes**: Small (<10k), Medium (10k-500k), Large (500k+)
3. **Rotate sets** - don't use identical hashtags every post
4. **Monitor banned hashtags** - Instagram flags some
5. **Track performance** - note which sets drive engagement
