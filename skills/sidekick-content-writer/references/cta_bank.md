## Platform-Specific Notes

**Instagram:**
- "Link in bio" (no clickable links in captions)
- Save CTAs boost algorithm
- Comments drive engagement score

**Facebook:**
- Direct links allowed in captions
- Share CTAs work well
- Questions drive comments

**GBP:**
- Direct action: "Call now", "Visit website"
- Keep CTAs business-focused
- Include contact information
