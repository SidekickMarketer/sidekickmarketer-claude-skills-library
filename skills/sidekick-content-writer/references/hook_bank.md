## Question Hooks

Drive curiosity and engagement:

```
What's the #1 mistake new [X] make?
Why do most people fail at [X]?
Did you know this about [X]?
Ever wonder why [X] is so hard?
What would you do if [scenario]?
Can you guess what happened next?
Ready for a hard truth about [X]?
```

**Examples:**
- "What's the #1 mistake new piano students make?"
- "Why do most home practice sessions fail?"
- "Did you know this about your HVAC system?"

---

## Statement Hooks

Bold claims that stop the scroll:

```
This changed everything for [X].
Most people get this completely wrong.
Here's what nobody tells you about [X].
The secret to [X] isn't what you think.
This is the post I wish I'd seen [X] ago.
Unpopular opinion: [statement].
Stop doing [X]. Here's why.
```

**Examples:**
- "This changed everything for our student Marcus."
- "Most beginners get practice completely wrong."
- "Here's what nobody tells you about learning guitar."

---

## Story Hooks

Draw readers into a narrative:

```
3 months ago, [name] couldn't [X].
I'll never forget the moment [X].
When [name] first walked in, they [X].
It started with a simple question: [X].
Last week, something incredible happened.
[Name] almost gave up. Then [X].
The day everything clicked for [X].
```

**Examples:**
- "3 months ago, Sarah couldn't play a single chord."
- "I'll never forget the moment Emma played her first song."
- "When Marcus first walked in, he was terrified."

---

## Pattern Interrupt Hooks

Break expectations:

```
Forget everything you know about [X].
Plot twist: [unexpected statement].
Wait for it...
Okay, we need to talk about [X].
This isn't your typical [X] post.
Read this before you [common action].
```

**Examples:**
- "Forget everything you know about practice."
- "Plot twist: talent isn't the secret to success."
- "This isn't your typical music lesson promo."

---
