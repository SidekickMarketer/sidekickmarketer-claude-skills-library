# LinkedIn Performance Benchmarks 2025

## Engagement Rate Benchmarks

| Tier | Engagement Rate | What It Means |
|------|-----------------|---------------|
| Below Average | <2% | Content needs improvement |
| Average | 2-4% | Typical performance |
| Good | 4-6% | Outperforming most |
| Excellent | 6-8% | Top performer territory |
| Viral | >8% | Exceptional content |

**Calculation:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Impressions x 100

## Follower Growth Benchmarks

| Profile Type | Monthly Growth | Notes |
|--------------|----------------|-------|
| New profile (<1K) | 20-30% | Rapid early growth |
| Building (1K-5K) | 10-15% | Steady growth phase |
| Established (5K-10K) | 5-10% | Quality over quantity |
| Influencer (10K+) | 3-5% | Maintaining momentum |

## Content Format Performance

| Format | Avg Engagement | Avg Reach | Best Use |
|--------|----------------|-----------|----------|
| Text posts | 3.5% | Medium | Stories, insights |
| Carousels | 4.2% | High | Educational content |
| Polls | 5.8% | Very High | Engagement boost |
| Native video | 3.8% | Medium-High | Personal connection |
| External links | 1.2% | Low | Avoid in main post |
| Documents (PDF) | 4.5% | High | Detailed how-tos |

## Posting Frequency Benchmarks

| Posting Frequency | Expected Results |
|-------------------|------------------|
| 1x/week | Minimal growth |
| 2-3x/week | Steady growth |
| 4-5x/week | Optimal for most |
| Daily | Top performer territory |
| 2x/day | Diminishing returns risk |

## Hashtag Performance

| # of Hashtags | Impact on Reach |
|---------------|-----------------|
| 0 | Baseline |
| 1-3 | +10-15% |
| 4-5 | +5-10% |

| 10+ | Negative |

| Long, value-add replies | Very positive |
| Reply + question | Highest positive |
