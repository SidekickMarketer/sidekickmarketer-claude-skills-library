### Format Guidelines
**Text Posts:**
- Optimal: 600-1,200 characters
- Use white space (+25% performance)
- Strong closing statement (+20-40% engagement)

**Carousels:**
- 6-10 slides optimal
- Hook on slide 1
- One idea per slide
- CTA on final slide

**Video:**
- 60-90 seconds max
- Vertical format
- Captions required
- Hook in first 3 seconds
