# LinkedIn Headline Formulas

## Character Limit
**220 characters maximum** (but only ~60 visible on mobile before truncation)

Put the most important info first.

## Proven Formulas

### 1. Role + Who You Help + Outcome
```
[Title] | Helping [audience] [achieve outcome]
```
**Example:** "CEO @ Reveal | Helping radiologists transition away from gadolinium contrast agents"

### 2. Role + Specific Expertise
```
[Title] at [Company] | [Specific expertise area]
```
**Example:** "Founder @ Sidekick Marketer | LinkedIn Strategy for B2B Founders"

### 3. Role + Value Proposition
```
[Title] | [What you do] for [who]
```
**Example:** "Marketing Director | Building demand gen engines for SaaS startups"

### 4. Role + Credibility + Focus
```
[Title] @ [Company] | [Credibility marker] | [Focus area]
```
**Example:** "CEO @ BioTech Inc | 20+ Years in Pharma | Building the Future of Diagnostics"

### 5. Mission-Driven
```
[Title] | On a mission to [mission]
```
**Example:** "Founder & CEO | On a mission to make music education accessible to every child"

### 6. Results-Focused
```
[Title] | [Specific result achieved]
```
**Example:** "Growth Lead | Scaled 3 startups from $0 to $10M ARR"

### 7. Dual Role
```
[Role 1] + [Role 2] | [Connecting thread]
```
**Example:** "Scientist + CEO | Translating research into life-saving diagnostics"

## What NOT to Do

❌ Just job title: "CEO"
❌ Buzzwords without substance: "Visionary Thought Leader"
❌ Too vague: "Helping businesses grow"
❌ All about you, nothing for them: "Award-winning executive with 20 years experience"
❌ Hashtag stuffing: "#marketing #sales #leadership #entrepreneur"

## Formula by Goal

| Goal | Best Formula |
|------|--------------|
| Lead generation | Role + Who You Help + Outcome |
| Thought leadership | Role + Specific Expertise |
| Job seeking | Role + Results + What You Want |
| Brand building | Mission-Driven or Role + Value Prop |
| Recruiting | Role + Company + Culture Hook |

## Company Page Tagline

**120 characters maximum**

Formulas:
- "[What we do] for [who]"
- "The [category] company that [differentiator]"
- "[Outcome] for [audience] through [method]"

**Examples:**
- "MRI contrast agents without the risks of gadolinium"
- "Full-service marketing for small businesses that want to grow"
- "Music education that builds confident, creative kids"
